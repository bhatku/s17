Hypertension

Traditionally Indian recipes utilize lot of salt and soda, which wasn’t bad until the time people were indulged in hard-physical-labor and sweating a lot. However, nowadays workplaces and bedrooms are becoming air-conditioned, which is leading to sodium buildup causing elevation in blood pressure. The situation gets further complicated in fatty persons. The medicines that are prescribed to regulate the blood pressure often work by slowing-down the heart-rate, limiting the blood supply to the body. Eliminating the sodium buildup and fat deposits can lead to clearer-vessels and easier-to-flow-thinner-blood. Sehatvan’s intervention through body, mind, food and environment may help achieve this in 2 to 4 weeks.
