About the retreat:
In this fast-paced life, it is good to occasionally take a step back, look inside, listen to what your body is telling you and take some time off to rejuvenate. The life of our ancestors was intricately interlaced with nature and they looked to the earth for wisdom and healing. Over time, we have lost this ability and as we move further away from the wilderness, we are facing issues with our mental and physical health. The inside out youth retreat is an initiative by Sehatvan to hold space for internal and external healing amidst nature.

Our retreats are rooted with love and are a potboiler of people from various arenas of life. We are looking to have discussions on wellness and offering ways to live a life that is less stressful. We wish to find answers here for simpler living and hope you leave from here stronger, readier and happier.
There have been extensive researches conducted on the direct effect of nature and well-being, here we put it up for the test, letting you decide what you wish to take with you. We invite people to shed their inhibitions and like wilderness, let their true selves shine.
Sir David Attenborough said, “An understanding of the natural world and what’s in it is a source of not only a great curiosity but great fulfilment.”
So come, hug a tree, speak to the birds, count the stars and go back with your heart full of love.


Who is it for?
If you are looking to slow down, take a step forward and open yourself up for new experiences, this retreat is for you. We are looking for participants who wish to understand nature and all that it has to offer, people who are open to go back to simpler living habits and explore their response to such surroundings. The retreat holds space for everyone with an open mind and the will to explore.

What can you expect?
On a personal level, we wish for you to open up and have conversations about your life journeys, the meaning you find in the mundane and what makes you happy. We are looking to have discussions on health and how to find a balance between the various aspects of our lives. We will all cook and work on the farm together to get a holistic experience of living close to the earth. The retreat will have conversations on how to keep our youthfulness alive and ways to increase lifespan through small lifestyle changes.
Moreover, you can also look forward to a release of feel good, happy hormones as you eat freshly made dinner under the starlit skies.
